Yango Pro, RIDE, Feres, ZayRide, Safe, and Seregela. Yawatal reads whichever of these is currently on your screen and rates the trip request accordingly.
Yawatal uses Android's Accessibility Service solely to read the fare, distance, and duration text already visible on your screen from a supported driver app, so it can calculate a verdict. It does not tap, type, or take any action on your behalf, and it does not read screens from apps outside the supported list. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
