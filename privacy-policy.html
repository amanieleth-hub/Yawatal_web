1. Introduction

Yawatal Technologies ("we", "our", "us") operates the Yawatal (ያዋጣል) mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use Yawatal on your Android device.

Yawatal is a driver-assistance tool for professional ride-hailing drivers in Ethiopia. It is not a ride-hailing platform and does not provide transportation services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Phone number (OTP verification and account identification)
  • Full name, city, vehicle type, platforms operated, driving experience

Accessibility-Derived Data (only when enabled by you):

  • Fare amount on ride request screens
  • Pickup distance indicator
  • Destination zone or area name
  • Trip distance indicator

Location: Approximate GPS for pickup distance and zone classification. Processed on-device, not stored on servers.

Firebase Services Used:

Firebase Auth (Anonymous)Secure session without email/password
Cloud FirestoreDriver profile, subscription, OTP records
Crash AnalyticsAnonymised crash diagnostics only

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Credit card, banking, or financial account information
  • Personal messages, emails, or social media content
  • Screen content unrelated to ride request evaluation
  • Contacts, call logs, photos, camera, or microphone data

4. OTP Verification Policy

  • OTP codes expire after 5 minutes
  • OTP documents are permanently deleted immediately after successful verification
  • Failed attempts retain no data
  • You may request a new code at any time via Resend Code

5. Data Retention

OTP verification codesDeleted on verify or 5-min expiry
Driver profile (Firestore)Duration of active account
Subscription records12 months after expiry
SMS dispatch log30 days
Crash reports90 days, then purged
Local app dataUntil app uninstall

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly inside the app:

  • Open Yawatal → Settings → scroll to Danger Zone → tap Delete My Account
  • Confirm the deletion in the dialog — your account, trip history, and all stored data will be permanently removed

Alternatively, you can request deletion by email:

  • Email amanieleth@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include your registered phone number
  • We confirm within 48 hours and complete deletion within 7 business days

7. Data Security

  • All data in transit encrypted using TLS 1.2+
  • Firestore security rules limit write access to authenticated owner only
  • No accessibility screen content is stored on any server

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw Accessibility Services consent at any time via Android Settings
  • Lodge a complaint with relevant data protection authority in Ethiopia

Contact: amanieleth@gmail.com

9. Ethiopia Compliance & International Users

Yawatal operates primarily in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, and complies with applicable Ethiopian data protection principles including the Ethiopian Computer Crime Proclamation (No. 958/2016).

Firebase infrastructure may process data in Google LLC data centres under standard contractual clauses for international data transfers.
