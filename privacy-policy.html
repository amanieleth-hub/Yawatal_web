Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE — update before publishing]

This Privacy Policy explains what information Yawatal ("Yawatal," "we," "us") collects through the Yawatal Android application (the "App"), how it's used, and the choices available to you. It applies to drivers who install and use the App in Addis Ababa and elsewhere.

In short: Yawatal reads on-screen trip information from supported ride-hailing driver apps, on your device, solely to calculate a profitability verdict. It does not collect, store, or transmit the content of your screen, and it does not act on your behalf.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Account & authentication information

  • Phone number, used to send a one-time password (OTP) and verify your account.
  • Basic account details you provide during registration (such as name).
  • Subscription status and payment confirmation (Yawatal does not directly process or store full payment card details).

1.2 Accessibility Service data

Yawatal uses Android's Accessibility Service API to read on-screen text and button colors from a specific, supported list of ride-hailing driver apps (Yango Pro, RIDE, Feres, ZayRide, Safe, and Seregela) while those apps are open. This is used exclusively to identify trip details already visible on your screen — fare, distance, duration, and fare tier — in order to calculate a real-time verdict.

  • This data is processed on your device, in memory, to produce a verdict.
  • Yawatal does not read screen content from apps outside the supported list.
  • Yawatal does not use Accessibility Service data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to calculating your verdict.
  • Raw screen content is not stored or transmitted off your device as part of this process.

1.3 Trip & usage information

To power trip history and analytics features you choose to use, Yawatal stores the verdict, fare, distance, and duration figures already described above (not the raw screen content) associated with your account, so you can review past trips.

1.4 Device & diagnostic information

Standard technical information (such as device model, Android version, and app version) may be collected to diagnose crashes and maintain compatibility across devices.

2. How we use information

  • To calculate and display trip verdicts in real time.
  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription.
  • To provide trip history and analytics inside the App.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve reliability.
  • To respond to support requests you send us.

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use Accessibility Service data for advertising.

3. Where information is stored

Account, subscription, and trip-history information is stored using Firebase (a Google Cloud service), which acts as our data processor. Firebase's own handling of infrastructure-level data is governed by Google's data processing terms.

4. Third-party sharing

Yawatal does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only with:

  • Service providers who help operate the App (such as Firebase/Google Cloud), under obligations to protect your data.
  • Authorities, where required by applicable law.
  • A successor entity, in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer — with notice provided where required.

5. Data retention

Account and trip-history information is retained for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the App's features. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time — see Section 7.

6. Your choices & permissions

  • Accessibility Service permission can be turned off at any time in Android's system settings, though the App's core verdict feature will not function without it.
  • You can request a copy or deletion of your account data by contacting us (Section 7).
  • Notification, microphone, or other permissions are only requested if a specific feature needs them, and are explained at the time they're requested.

7. Contact & data requests

For privacy questions, or to request access to or deletion of your data, contact support@yawatal.com. We aim to respond to verified requests within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children's privacy

Yawatal is intended for licensed drivers and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
