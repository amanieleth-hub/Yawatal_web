1. Introduction

Yawatal Technologies ("we", "our", "us") operates the Yawatal (ያዋጣል) mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use Yawatal on your Android device.

Yawatal is a driver-assistance tool for professional ride-hailing drivers in Ethiopia. It is not a ride-hailing platform and does not provide transportation services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Phone number (OTP verification and account identification)
  • Full name, city, vehicle type, platforms operated, driving experience

Location: Approximate GPS for pickup distance and zone classification. Processed on-device, not stored on servers.

3. Accessibility Service and Screen-Reading Access

Why we request this permission

Yawatal uses the Android Accessibility Service to read trip-offer information displayed on your screen inside the ride-hailing driver apps you already have installed, so we can show you a real-time GREEN / YELLOW / RED recommendation before you accept or decline a trip. None of these platforms provide this information through any public API, webhook, or notification, so on-screen reading is the only way to access it.

Apps this applies to

Yango ProRIDEFeresSafe ZayRideSeregelaLittleTaxiye

Yawatal only reads on-screen content from these eight apps. It does not read content from any other app on your device.

What we read

  • Fare amount shown on the ride request screen
  • Pickup distance indicator
  • Trip distance indicator
  • Destination zone or area name

What we never do

Yawatal's use of Accessibility Service is read-only. It never taps, clicks, fills in forms, or otherwise interacts with any app on your behalf. It never accepts or declines a trip for you — every decision is made manually by you, inside your own ride-hailing app, exactly as it would work without Yawatal installed. It never reads messages, contacts, banking apps, photos, or any app outside the eight listed above.

What we do with this data

  • Processed on-device to compute a trip-quality score and overlay color
  • Stored under your authenticated account in Firestore to power your own trip history
  • Not sold, ever
  • Not shared with the ride-hailing platforms
  • Not shared with advertisers or data brokers
  • Not used to train third-party AI models

Retention

We keep your most recent 100 trip records. Once a new trip record is saved beyond your 100th, the oldest record is automatically deleted. You can also delete all trip history immediately via Settings → Danger Zone, or by requesting full account deletion (Section 7).

Your control

You can disable Accessibility permission at any time from Yawatal's own Settings screen or from Android Settings → Accessibility. Disabling it stops the overlay and trip-reading feature but does not delete your account or existing trip history.

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Credit card, banking, or financial account information
  • Personal messages, emails, or social media content
  • Screen content unrelated to ride request evaluation, or from any app outside the eight listed in Section 3
  • Contacts, call logs, photos, camera, or microphone data

5. OTP Verification Policy

  • OTP codes expire after 5 minutes
  • OTP documents are permanently deleted immediately after successful verification
  • Failed attempts retain no data
  • You may request a new code at any time via Resend Code

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly inside the app:

  • Open Yawatal → Settings → scroll to Danger Zone → tap Delete My Account
  • Confirm the deletion in the dialog — your account, trip history, and all stored data will be permanently removed

Alternatively, you can request deletion by email:

  • Email amanieleth@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include your registered phone number
  • We confirm within 48 hours and complete deletion within 7 business days

8. Data Security

  • All data in transit encrypted using TLS 1.2+
  • Firestore security rules limit write access to authenticated owner only
  • No accessibility screen content is stored on any server — only the parsed fare/distance/time/zone fields described in Section 3
