Features

Everything Yawatal does while you drive.

No new app to switch to mid-trip — Yawatal sits on top of the driver apps you already run and adds one clear signal.

Live verdicts

Real-time GREEN / YELLOW / RED overlay

A small floating badge appears over your driver app the moment a trip request lands, rating it on estimated ETB earned per minute after fuel — not just the fare on screen.

Multi-platform

One tool across six apps

Yango Pro, RIDE, Feres, ZayRide, Safe, and Seregela are all read the same way, so a "GREEN" means the same thing no matter which app the request came from.

Alerts

Sound and vibration cues

Each verdict has its own tone and vibration pattern, so you get the signal without having to look down at your phone in traffic.

History

Trip history & analytics

Review past trip verdicts and outcomes to see how your accept rate lines up with actual profitability over a day, a week, or a shift.

Under the hood

A transparent, rule-based decision engine.

No black box. Every verdict comes from a fixed, explainable calculation — not a model guessing on your behalf.

ETB per minute & per km

The core figure behind every verdict: what a trip pays once travel time is accounted for, compared to a minimum threshold.

Fuel-cost aware

Estimated fuel cost for the trip distance is factored in before a verdict is shown, so a long low-fare trip is treated differently from a short one.

Zone & fare-tier multipliers

Surge and mid-tier fares are recognized on screen and factored into the math, rather than treated the same as a standard fare.

Bilingual by default

English and አማርኛ, throughout.

Every verdict, alert, and setting is available in both languages, switchable at any time from the app.

Subscription-based

Straightforward access, no fare-based cut.

Yawatal runs on a simple subscription — see Pricing for current plans. It never takes a percentage of what you earn per trip.
